Welcome

Among all the ancient and unique temples of South India, Kalathil Vettakkorumakan Temple of Edathala, South India occupies the most significant position in the whole world. One can find the Edathala Vettakkorumakan temple along the eastern side of Ernakulam district, A tourist finds Vettakkorumakan temple about 20kms from Cochin town.

Edathala Vettakkorumakan Temple is dedicated to son of Lord Shiva and is known as vettakkorumakan in local language. Pilgrims flock to the vettakkorumakan temple during the Sabarimala season and Thirunal festival which is celebrated with great enthusiasm in the month of Makaram, makaram-makam, nashram, thiruna (Malayalavarsham).
